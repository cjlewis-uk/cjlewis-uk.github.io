E_counter – Calorie Control System

A structured daily system using fixed meals and movement to deliver consistent fat loss and strength retention with minimal decision making.

Daily Structure

  • 75g muesli + milk
  • 3 × protein + veg meals
  • Top up: skyr / yoghurt ± fruit

Movement

  • Walk daily
  • Weights (fixed routine)
  • 7 minute core

Targets

  • 1400–1700 kcal
  • ≥120g protein
  • ≥25g fibre

Rule: Execute daily. Adjust weekly
